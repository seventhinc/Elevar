Ujjivan is one of the most respected and the largest urban-focused microfinance institution in India


Company Overview

Launched in 2006 as the first urban-focused MFI in India, Ujjivan has expanded its operations over the past five years to cover 20 Indian states. Ujjivan’s accomplished management team has successfully combined microfinance methodologies with the technologies and efficiencies of modern retail banking. Ujjivan has built an institution that is best in class in all aspects: customer service, community involvement, innovation, efficiency, work place engagement, leadership, governance and reputation. Ujjivan has received the following awards: Best Company to Work for in 2011 (#1 in MFI Industry & #14 across all industries in India), Microfinance Organization of the Year, and the Mix Platinum Award for Social Performance Reporting.

It is estimated that the remaining microfinance market opportunity is $320 billion

Community Served

Ujjivan’s customers are economically active, industrious, hard-working women from India’s urban and semi-urban areas. Top occupations include: cloth sellers, shopkeepers, factory workers, flower/vegetable vendors, milk suppliers, house maids, traders and tailors. Ujjivan focuses on underserved markets.

Entrepreneur

Samit Ghosh, founder, has 30 +years of international banking experience (senior leadership at Citibank India, Standard Chartered, Bank Muscat, HDFC) and is the leading, senior voice in Indian microfinance today.

Elevar's Take

One of the strongest management team and business model in Indian microfinance, supporting the creation of a conservative bank, wholly focused on the long-term economic empowerment of the urban poor in India

Ujjivan presented Elevar, through both of its funds, with the opportunity to be a significant investor behind the most prudent and experienced management team in Indian microfinance fully focused on serving the urban poor. Through Fund I, Elevar represented the founding equity capital to launch the institution and Elevar has continued to support Ujjivan’s growth, participating in all of its subsequent equity rounds. Elevar’s contributions on the board have included helping the company to strategize around additional fundraising and selecting new investors, ensuring alignment between management, employees and shareholders on issues critical to the company’s growth and success, supporting the company’s long-term focus yet highly innovative productive development and astute approach to market development.

We share Samit Ghosh’s vision to create a suite of financial services for the urban poor through modern branches, professional staff and total integration with banking norms, while supporting his holistic framework. supporters of his holistic framework.

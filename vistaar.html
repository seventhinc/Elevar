Vistaar specializes exclusively in providing credit to rural and semi-urban small businesses.


Company Overview

Vistaar designs and delivers customized loan products that address the individual needs of small businesses in terms of loan amount and tenor. Vistaar targets the dominant livelihoods in specific markets, e.g. power looms/textiles, and creates financial products to address the specific needs of that industry. One of the company’s core strengths lies in its solid, experienced, and visionary leadership: Vistaar’s founders have more than 55 years of combined experience across commercial, agriculture and rural banking, technology, and operations.

SMEs Credit Gap in Emerging Markets is over $2.1 Trillion

Community Served

There are 26+ million micro/small/medium enterprises (MSMEs) employing 60+ million people in India. Large, vibrant clusters of micro‐enterprises focused on one particular livelihood can be found within a geographic area – e.g. power loom textiles in Tamil Nadu or stone carving in Rajasthan. These enterprises lack access to appropriately designed and priced financial services. They rely on moneylenders, suppliers and buyers for working capital funding at high interest rates.

However, Shubham has focused on opening branches in urban and peri-urban low income communities with high stability (stable base of residents for multiple years) and high degrees of entrepreneurialism. Entrepreneurs

Brahmanand Hegde, CEO, is a 30 year veteran of rural banking and agricultural lending in India. He was director of Fullerton India’s rural banking since 2007. Prior to that, Brahmanand ran ICICI’s rural banking group after many years in ICICI’s Agri Business Division.

Ramakrishna Nisthala, COO, has over 25 years of experience in sales and marketing and business strategy. He ran Fullerton India’s rural banking group since 2008 and was instrumental prior to that in the rollout of Fullerton India’s 800 branch network. Previously, he led sales, marketing, strategic planning and implementation within several divisions of the Eicher Group.

Elevar's Take

Formidable management team with unique focus on attractive and underserved MSME market in India - with a unique cash flow based underwriting model that drew on industry / vertical expertise

Given our experience with microcredit, Elevar understood the opportunity to offer larger loan sizes and more customized products to successful entrepreneurs, who have larger enterprises than the typical microcredit client in India. Working with Brahmanand and Ramakrishna, who had piloted larger loan sizes within their microcredit efforts for Fullerton India, we co-developed a business plan for Vistaar that was solely focused on the “missing middle.” Vistaar deepened its understanding of its four initial key livelihoods and has scaled their operation in these key livelihoods by leveraging this expertise. Vistaar today is a leading player in MSME finance in India.

Elevar was a founding investor at Vistaar - and also committed capital through a phase of crisis in the industry (triggered by the microfinance crisis in India) to help the company pivot its business model successfully.

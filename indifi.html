 Elevar’s Entry in 2016

indifi.com

Indifi is helping MSMEs thrive and grow, by providing a technology based platform to bridge the critical financing gaps faced by this segment.


Company Overview

Indifi is transforming the lending process for MSME borrowers in India. They have automated the traditional information gathering processes and underwriting principles, by making it far more effective and relevant to institutional lenders. These interventions significantly reduce the turnaround time for disbursals and improve accessibility to institutional credit for MSME borrowers.

65% of MSME borrowers do not have access to institutional credit.

Community Served

Indian MSMEs who are vendors for fast growing segments such as e-commerce, travel commerce, online B2B marketplaces and other small scale retailers or traders. MSMEs revenues are informal in nature and they lack access to collateral. Due to these constraints, they do not have access to institutional credit and therefore depend on expensive and untimely credit from unsecured lenders.

Elevar's Take

A tech driven model that bridges critical financing gaps for MSMEs by tapping into various industry supply chains to gather underwriting critical data (previously unavailable to lenders) – offers a massive opportunity with non-linear growth potential in financial services

Indifi is transforming the lending process for MSME borrowers in India. Their interventions improve accessibility to institutional credit for this borrower segment and significantly reduces the turnaround time for disbursals, thus effectively reducing their overall cost of borrowing. Elevar believes that this business model has the potential to unlock deep clusters of underwriting relevant customer information that remains disaggregated – towards enabling access to capital for MSMEs who have tremendous growth potential. The model has been thoughtfully constructed to leverage several recent market trends like e-commerce, as well as focusing on ‘offline’ supply chain aggregators that have existed in various industry verticals for several years and have strong historical relationships with these MSMEs. Lenders are keen to access this untapped segment and the team, with deep rooted experience in technology as well as financial services, is well positioned to execute this model.

Financiera TFC

FOUNDED IN 1997
 Elevar’s Entry in 2016
Board Member: Johanna Posada
tfc.com.pe

Financiera TFC is a regulated financial institution in Peru focused primarily on financing low-income housing and micro and small enterprises in second and third-tier cities.



Company Overview

TFC focuses on underserved markets with low penetration by the Peruvian financial sector. The company offers a diverse portfolio of financial products concentrating mainly on mortgage products to low-income families and financing for MSMEs. With this multi-product strategy, TFC aims to grow into a full-fledged large national bank serving low-income communities in the provinces of Peru.

It is estimated that the housing deficit in Peru is close to 1.9 Mn units, of which 1.3 Mn are needed outside of Lima, primarily in the low-income sector.

Community Served

Low and low-middle-income clients from 2nd and 3rd cities in Peru. About 2/3 of Peruvians live outside the Lima region, encompassing a great percentage of the low-income housing market and MSMEs in the country, that do not have the opportunity to obtain financial products from traditional banking players.

Entrepreneur

Andres Muñoz, Chairman of the Board, is recognized as one of the best bankers in Peru, with impressive strategic vision, excellent structuring capabilities, and deep execution skills. His 30-year career spans senior positions in the largest banks in Peru where he played a key role in innovating and serving new customers, especially in housing finance and agri-business finance.

Elevar's Take

Scalable client-centric business model in a large market that has the potential of impacting an underserved market in Peru

TFC is Elevar’s second investment from Elevar Equity III in Latin America and follows the fund’s thesis of addressing underserved communities through viable business models. Despite its size, TFC has the entrepreneurial spirit and innovative, customer focused culture of a much smaller organization. Andres and his team have managed to create a client-centric business model that truly embraces the idea of developing solutions for their client needs.

We believe that TFC is the best-positioned financial institution to address the financing needs of low-income housing segment in Peru, as well as other financing needs in second and third tier cities - a large, underbanked market that traditional players have failed to serve adequately due to lack of appropriate team, size, and expertise.

GloboKasNet’s vision is to turn into reality the economic and social inclusion of any person, anywhere, without regard to distance or population density.


Company Overview

GloboKasNet (GKN) is the fastest-growing, independent, multibank payment network in Peru offering transaction, processing and settlement services to low income urban and rural clients of banks, microfinance institutions (MFIs) and commercial wholesalers. GKN empowers its Agents – typically carefully selected small shop owners or merchants – with training and technology to perform all the functions of a bank teller or ATM, providing greater convenience and accessibility to end clients. Founded by seasoned telecommunications executives, Gino Picasso and Michael Deutschman, GKN has global ambitions and launched operations in Peru where it has a nationwide Agent network.

The Peruvian financial system processes over 57 million transactions per month

Community Served

GKN services low income urban and rural Peruvians who have difficulty accessing bank/MFI branches to complete their simple banking transactions due to inconvenient locations, limited operating hours, or long lines.

However, Shubham has focused on opening branches in urban and peri-urban low income communities with high stability (stable base of residents for multiple years) and high degrees of entrepreneurialism. An improved home has direct and positive impact on the health, security and self-esteem of homeowners and their families.

Entrepreneurs

Gino, Co-Founder and CEO, firmly believes that bank agents have the potential to significantly increase the financial access of disconnected communities to a range of formal financial services, including savings, payments and transfers, and insurance. He has over 25 years of experience in global information and communications technology, most recently as CEO of Mobile 365 and Iridium Satellite. Previously he held senior positions at GE and McKinsey. He brings significant experience operating early stage, growth and mature companies internationally.

Michael, Co-Founder and COO, is passionate about the idea of offering disconnected communities a convenient and affordable opportunity to cash-in and cash-out services. He was formerly the Chief Counsel and Chief Administrative Officer of Iridium Satellite after a long career in law and real estate/venture capital investing.

Elevar's Take

Superior management team and compelling business model in ideal regulatory environment allowed GKN to capitalize on fundamental network dynamics.

GKN was one of the most advanced agents focused payments companies operating at the base of the economic pyramid. Elevar’s understanding of the ideal circumstances for payments networks to succeed – in terms of regulatory environment supporting interoperability across banks, technology / telecommunications infrastructure and business model – led us to make an investment in GKN in the Latin Americal context as against more nascent payments companies in India and other countries with suboptimal conditions. Elevar recognized the value in a seasoned telecommunications expert launching ground operations in Peru with a respected local team.

As the Peruvian operations continue to grow and achieve profitability, GKN will turn its attention to investing in other countries and offer service platforms like extended banking services through alternative distribution channels (eg : loan origination, insurance sales to SMEs etc) and e-commerce enablement.

Elevar invested in GKN at a positive inflection point in the business and has been a key stakeholder since.

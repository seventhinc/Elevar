Finae

FOUNDED IN 2009
 Elevar’s Entry in 2014
Board Member: Johanna Posada
finae.com

FINAE provides education loans to low income and lower-middle income students in Mexico pursuing advanced studies.


Company Overview

FINAE empowers young adults with professional aspirations by offering student loans that cover tuition at high-quality institutions with high probability of job placement upon graduation. These loans cover up to 100% of tuition at affordable monthly payments that make higher education accessible to many.

Only 7% of young adults aged 19-24 from low and lower-middle income households go to college compared to 87% of their peers from high-income households.

Community Served

Mexico has 12.8 million individuals aged 19-24. 70% belong to low and lower-middle income households, with only 7% in this group attending college compared to 87% of high-income individuals in this age range.

Entrepreneur

Francisco Vizcaya, FINAE’s CEO, brings a wealth of experience from his executive roles at Banco Santander, GE Capital, Banamex, among many others. His operational experience, especially in the structuring of innovative financial products, has been highly leveraged in FINAE’s product design and securitization strategy. He is a recognized executive in the banking community, which allows him to attract multiple sources of funding and top-caliber talent to the company. His vision is for FINAE to become the leading bank in the education sector, not just in Mexico, but perhaps in greater Latin America, and he has the energy and passion to make this a reality.

Elevar's Take

Proven, scalable business model in a nascent market that has the potential of impacting thousands of lives of low and lower-middle income students in Mexico

FINAE is Elevar’s first investment from Elevar Equity III in Latin America and follows the fund’s thesis of serving underserved communities through viable business models. FINAE has a deep customer understanding, innovative product structuring, and a vision to capture the nascent student financing market in Mexico. The impact generated by FINAE will not only materialize in the life of the student going to college, but also in the lives of their families and the generations to come after.

Francisco and his team have demonstrated an aptitude for adapting to the needs of the consumer; continuously innovating to better serve these communities. FINAE has been built on the premise that the company will serve a large customer base and the team has tirelessly focused on perfecting its products, processes, and market approach to create a platform that can be utilized for years to come. FINAE’s goal is to grow at an accelerated pace and expand into new geographies. Elevar is looking forward to supporting the team as they continue to strengthen and grow FINAE and help them make higher education affordable to all in Mexico and beyond.

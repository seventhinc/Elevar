"There is no doubt that entrepreneurs can create scalable and sustainable approaches to socio-economic development."

Vipul Rawal

Vipul joined Elevar in 2011. He believes that: “There is no doubt that entrepreneurs can create scalable and sustainable approaches to socio-economic development. Consequently, Elevar became an obvious journey for me to participate in catalysing the efforts of these entrepreneurs.”

Prior to joining Elevar, Vipul worked with Tholons Capital where he was managing a small fund put together by the founders that had a diversified investment strategy while also focusing on start-ups in the IT/BPO space. He also assisted some of the private equity clients of Tholons with their investments. Prior to that, he worked with Goldman Sachs in the Investment Banking Division where he focused on the Financial Institutions Group. He has provided pro-bono help to social entrepreneurs on business strategy and fund raising and has also committed some of his time over the weekends with a group of friends to foster social enterprise start-ups in Bangalore. He also currently serves as an independent director on the board of MadRat Games, a developer of learning games.

Vipul has an MBA in Finance & Marketing from The International Management Institute, Europe and a Post-Graduation Diploma in Planning & Entrepreneurship from The Indian Institute of Planning and Management, Bangalore. He also holds a B.Sc. from Bangalore University. Vipul has a Black Belt and is a Karate Coach. He has a strong interest in teaching and mentoring. He serves as guest faculty at a few business schools and is a mentor with the National Entrepreneurship Network. Vipul is also enthusiastic about Microsoft Excel, technology and social media and enjoys city farming in his free time.

SecureNow

Founded in 2011
 Elevar’s Entry in 2016
Board Member: Sandeep Farias
securenow.in

SecureNow provides general insurance to SMEs with a technology enabled proposition that is core to its distribution and customer service.


Company Overview

SecureNow’s unique business model provides customized general insurance solutions to SMEs in India through a web-based platform using technology for customer acquisition and servicing. The approach is supported by strong offline presence to build and sustain customer relationships.

General insurance penetration in India is less than 1% of GDP.

Community Served

SecureNow caters to Indian SMEs that are highly under-insured. SMEs lack access to organized institutional brokers and are served by a fragmented and unorganized market of individual insurance brokers and agents. This results in poor quality services and response rates. With its customer focus and understanding, SecureNow aims to deliver increased convenience and better value to the underserved SME segment.

Entrepreneurs

Kapil Mehta, Co-founder brings over two decades of experience across multiple verticals, including insurance, consulting and operations in retail businesses – building a deep understanding of the Indian consumer. He started his career at Unilever and has worked at McKinsey & Company and Max New York Life Insurance. Prior to becoming an entrepreneur, Kapil was the managing director of DLF Pramerica Life Insurance, a role he was deputed to by Prudential Financial.

Abhishek Bondia, Co-founder has worked at McKinsey & Company. He has deep experience in the P&C, health insurance and health care markets in the US and India. Abhishek has built extensive expertise in multiple insurance products. He is a rank-holding Chartered Accountant by education.

Elevar's Take

A thoughtfully constructed, scalable business model which has the potential to transform distribution of general insurance products to SMEs that lack access to quality risk management products critical for their businesses

SecureNow is the first entrepreneurial effort in India at setting up an SME focused broking entity. They are uniquely positioned to lead the market in terms of catering to this segment. Large insurance brokers do not have the DNA or inclination, given small ticket sizes, to be able to pursue the SME segment which requires a quasi ‘retail’ distribution culture. Further, the individual brokers and agents lack the sophistication and infrastructure to provide a robust proposition to the small businesses.

The combination of the promoters’ experience, execution abilities, passion for the SME segment, market reputation built thus far and in-depth understanding of the nuances of insurance products make this a compelling investment proposition for Elevar. The entrepreneurs share Elevar’s ambition for scale and providing the SMEs the bargaining power and choice that has traditionally belonged only to large corporates in the country. This has the potential to fundamentally transform the way insurance has been distributed in India.

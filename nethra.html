“An Elevar entrepreneur is one who builds a scalable, transformational business that enable a customer to gain access to the formal ecosystem.”

Nethra Bhat

Nethra joined Elevar in 2015 and works with the investment team in Bangalore. She believes that: “an Elevar entrepreneur is one who is able to build a scalable and transformational business around a customer to enable him/her to gain access into the formal ecosystem. It is a great learning experience to see the entrepreneurs through this journey.”

Nethra comes with close to a decade of experience across different streams of financial services. Prior to joining Elevar, she worked with IFMR Capital in the debt space involved in raising debt for clients in the small business lending and housing finance sectors, building a framework for the internal credit assessment and structuring debt products. In her previous roles at CRISIL GR&A and Amba Research, she led a team that provided research support to European and US-based asset management companies covering multiple sectors across different geographies.

Nethra graduated from Bangalore University with a Bachelor in Commerce degree and is a Chartered Financial Analyst (USA) and ACCA fellow (UK). Artistically inclined, she enjoys painting and pottery in her free time. She also loves traveling and makes sure she sees one new place every year.

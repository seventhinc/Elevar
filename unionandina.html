Union Andina

FOUNDED IN 2005
 Elevar’s Entry in 2011
Board Member: Johanna Posada
union-andina.net

Union Andina is an innovative company offering housing purchase and finance solutions to migrant workers from Latin America.


Company Overview

Union Andina enables Latin American migrants in the United States and Europe to purchase a home in their country of origin. It achieves this by offering migrant workers access to qualified real estate developers’ inventory as well as mortgage financing solutions. Union Andina allows migrants to redirect remittance cash flows that are otherwise spent on consumption into investments in valuable real assets, benefiting their entire families and allowing them to realize lifelong dreams.

Remittances to emerging markets totaled $351 billion in 2011

Community Served

There are over 215 million migrants estimated worldwide, of which 30 million are from Latin American. Migrants from Latin America drive a $60 billion remittances industry, of which only 5% is used for housing. Latin American migrants have substantial housing needs and a majority of them express the desire to buy a home in their country of origin. Union Andina aims to drive up the share of remittances allocated to home ownership. Migrant workers relocate to improve their family’s well-being and increase their personal net worth. They often work 3 or 4 jobs in order to realize their dream to improve the quality of life of their family members in their home country.

Entrepreneur

Andy Altena, a migrant himself, understands the value and importance of serving the migrant communities and has a genuine commitment and passion to help migrants achieve their goals of owning a home. He is an experienced financial executive with substantial banking and loan structuring experience in Europe and Latin America. Andy was the Director of Derivatives Products for ABN AMRO in Spain, CFO and member of the Executive Committee at Banco Santander in Peru, and has been a successful entrepreneur.

Elevar's Take

Opportunity to partner with a very successful entrepreneur to provide an essential service to overlooked migrant communities

Union Andina provides an essential and empowering service to migrant workers who face significant barriers to access traditional financial services. We found Union Andina as we developed Elevar’s investment theme focused on migrant services. Elevar recognized the commitment and unique approach that Union Andina brings to migrants with dreams of owning a home and Andy and his team’s strong banking and commercial experience, a necessary component to run a complex organization.

Union Andina is building a mortgage services network that will grow in three ways: 1) adding additional sales locations in the U.S. and Europe; 2) increasing coverage of additional nationalities and 3) providing more services to migrants. Elevar believes that by growing and deepening its network and product offerings, Union Andina can become a general services platform for international migrant workers

Samunnati

FOUNDED IN 2014
 Elevar’s Entry in 2015
Board Member: Jyotsna Krishnan
samfin.in

Samunnati provides financial services to supply chain verticals lacking access to capital primarily in the agri and allied activities sector.


Company Overview

Samunnati addresses the massive demand for financial services to agriculture and allied supply chains that the banking channel has historically struggled to service. Samunnati integrates customer sourcing with supply chain financing, assessing creditworthiness keeping local dynamics in mind, making it a unique, differentiated offering in poorly served markets.

Total debt potential for just dairy and crop loans in India is $35 billion currently.

Community Served

Samunnati caters to underserved entrepreneurs at various levels of supply chains – like input suppliers, producers, processors and aggregators — by providing financial products that will improve efficiency and productivity through access to quicker and cheaper

Entrepreneur

Anil Kumar has over two decades of experience in the banking and the micro finance industry and is personally passionate about agriculture. His experience developing business models catering to the low income segment in this sector is particularly relevant for this venture along with his longstanding career in retail banking.

Elevar's Take

A highly scalable business model that focuses on leveraging the natural (local) dynamics in the agri-supply chains in its product design and distribution - in order to scale as well as risk-mitigate - providing much needed capital to individuals in these supply chains.

Samunnati is Elevar's first investment focused on agriculture in India. This investment leverages the inherent scalability of financial services business models in a sector that has so far been underserved by banks. Anil's deep understanding of this sector has enabled him to design an extremely customer centric proposition that gains from local supply chain dynamics both for business development as well as for risk mitigation. Elevar believes in the massive potential of this sector and has identified several pockets of opportunities through multiple field visits over the years. We believe if executed well, this investment could pave the way for the emergence of several similar players in agriculture in the years to come, bringing much needed access to capital and establishing the credibility of this customer segment.

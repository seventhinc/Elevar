Caja Rural Los Andes

 Elevar’s Entry in 2010
Board Member: Johanna Posada
cajarurallosandes.com

Los Andes vision is to create a leading microfinance institution of scale, and the preeminent provider of financial services in Peru’s rural market.


Company Overview

Los Andes is a profitable, deposit-taking, regulated bank that caters to the needs of the rural community in the southeast Andean region of Peru. It is positioned as a leading community bank with a recognizable, trust-worthy brand, strong customer focus, cost-efficient culture and a unique credit model focused on high quality, under-served rural customers.

The Peruvian microfinance credit market is estimated at $6 billion

Community Served

Micro-entrepreneurs/enterprises operating in dynamic markets driven by internal demand growth and living in robust communities with deep culture of entrepreneurship & cohesive social and economic ties. This includes male and female producers involved in livestock, trout farming, cheese making and other non-farm agricultural activities. Generally, they are involved in several economic activities to diversify income.

Entrepreneur

After leaving Peru to successfully pursue a long professional journey in international finance, Rosanna has come back to her Peruvian roots. She is extremely excited about the opportunity to impact the lives of micro-entrepreneurs in the Andean region of Peru. Rosanna has over 20 years of experience in international banking (senior management at Citigroup’s global consumer banking) and in Latin America investment banking (UBS). Rosanna is a Member of the Board of Grameen Foundation where she is currently Chairman of the Latin American Council and also serves as a Member of the Wharton Executive Board for Latin America and a Member of the Board of Enseña Perú, the Peruvian chapter of Teach for America.

Elevar's Take

Unique acquisition opportunity, immediate creation of intrinsic value and strong future growth, return and exit potential

Los Andes presented Elevar with the opportunity to be a significant investor and market player in Peru’s dynamic, coveted microfinance sector. The long-standing and productive relationship that Elevar has built with PDG and the Peruvian banking regulator provided us with access to a unique acquisition target at the right time. Elevar’s expertise in due diligence, negotiation and structuring, in conjunction with PDG’s local presence, banking experience and relationships, resulted in the successful execution of a very complex transaction.

Elevar was able to recognize the opportunity- a high quality team, loan portfolio, credit methodology and brand, as well as a unique client base. By eliminating disreputable shareholders, cleaning up the balance sheet and establishing new governance, immediate value was created. Over the past year, by working with the Los Andes management team, Elevar and PDG have turned Los Andes into an outstanding MFI. Although small, Los Andes is the optimal platform to establish Peru’s preeminent rural bank.

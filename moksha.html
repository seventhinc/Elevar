Company Overview

MokshaYug Access (Bagalkot and Kolar) is a rural supply chain management company that builds market linkages between rural communities and larger commercial markets with a focus on dairy.

Moksha Yug Access is promoting rural economic opportunity by building trading infrastructure and key market linkages between rural communities and larger commercial markets. Aiming to address the needs of the world’s poorest citizens, Moksha Yug Access operates on the premise that the rural poor – as producers, entrepreneurs and consumers– should be active participants in a growing economy.

Entrepreneur

Harsha is committed to making the rural poor active participants in India’s growth story. He has 14 years of investment and operating experience in the US, UK and India in Private Equity, Telecom, Infrastructure, Hydrocarbon and Agribusiness sectors.

Prior to starting-up MYA in 2006, he spent three years in London working in private equity and project development where he was involved with transactions in hydrocarbon & infrastructure sectors helping build a portfolio of high-quality infrastructure assets diversified across different stages of the asset life cycle.

He has three years of work experience in India, working for India’s largest private sector company where he was part of a core team which evaluated opportunities in the telecom sector and laid the blue print for the rollout of one of the world’s largest telecom networks. He also spent four years in the USA with Agribusiness and Investment Research companies.Harsha has been recognized as an outstanding and dynamic leader by the Asia Society’s 21 Young Leaders Initiative. He holds an MBA in International Business from Thunderbird, School of Global Management (USA) and Bachelors in Commerce from St. Joseph’s College of Commerce, Bangalore University.

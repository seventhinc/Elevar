Glocal Healthcare

FOUNDED IN 2010
 Elevar’s Entry in 2011
Board Member: Sandeep Farias
ghspl.com

Glocal provides quality, affordable, patient-centric healthcare services to rural India.


Company Overview

Focusing initially on locations in West Bengal, Glocal is setting up hospitals in rural India that offer out-patient, in-patient, operation theatre, emergency and obstetric services. Founder, Dr. Sabahat Azim believes in using empirical research to understand the unique health requirements of rural Indians and in innovating on the healthcare delivery model to bring health services within reach of rural India.

In India, 75% of the population lives in rural areas that lack access to basic healthcare services.

Community Served

The communities that Glocal serves consist primarily of low income, rural Indians. They have a real capacity and desire to pay for quality healthcare, but at present there is a dearth of quality options to meet demand. Through empirical on the ground research, Glocal’s management found that approximately 17 diseases constitute 85% and 42 diseases make up 95% of the disease load in rural India. By designing a facility focused on addressing these conditions and protocolizing healthcare, Glocal has dramatically brought down the capital expenditure cost per hospital and the cost of service delivery to patients.

Entrepreneurs

Dr.Azim, CEO, is a trained medical doctor. He is also a former officer in the Indian government (holding various positions including director of information technology and secretary to the Chief Minister of Tripura) and a tested entrepreneur, having built a pan-India, rural information and communications technology venture for one of India’s largest infrastructure players.

Mr. M. Damodaran, Glocal’s Chairman and Co-Founder, has held multiple senior positions in India’s state and central government and most significantly is a former Chairman of the Securities and Exchange Board of India, widely acknowledged as among India’s foremost champions of corporate governance. He was also the managing director of Unit Trust of India, and served as Health Secretary for the state of Tripura.

Elevar's Take

Innovative, patient-centric business model that provides superior value at lower cost; execution focused, knowledgeable management team

Glocal’s innovative view towards healthcare delivery for all Indians involves a strong focus on rural patients that is grounded in empirical research. It also provides a unique and thoughtful approach towards lowering the cost of service for rural communities. By focusing on the 42 conditions that make up 95% of disease load, the company has cut capital expenditure requirements by more than 50% and is able to reduce the cost of service to patients significantly.

Since Elevar’s investment, the company has proven its ability to hire experienced staff for its hospitals, ensuring that doctors and nurses will deliver high quality service to patients. Moreover, the company has leveraged management’s relationships with commercial banks to raise significant debt at startup phase. Glocal’s management is highly dedicated and capable of managing scale. In the case of Glocal’s first hospital, management opened doors to patients in less than 4 months from the start of construction. Glocal has demonstrated its ability to deliver quality healthcare at low cost to rural communities.

 Elevar’s Entry in 2016

afluenta.com

Afluenta is a technology-enabled marketplace that connects lenders and borrowers, increasing access and affordability of financial services in Latin America.


Company Overview

Afluenta is a technology-enabled marketplace that connects lenders and borrowers over its proprietary, secure and user friendly platform. By cutting out the traditional financial service providers and middlemen, the company delivers attractive yields to lenders/investors and lower-cost, convenient loans to the borrowers. Afluenta has the potential to majorly disrupt traditional banking services and create significant impact, especially in Latin American countries where financial intermediation has been amongst the most inefficient in the world, resulting in exorbitant borrowing rate, few attractive alternatives for both investors and borrowers and low penetration rates.

By 2025 $1 Trillion in loans will be originated through an online marketplace.

Community Served

The company impacts mid-low income, working class communities, improving lives through accessible and efficient consumer credit loans. It helps borrowers reduce their financial expenses, fund their business, improve their homes, invest in education and meet other needs and aspirations. Afluenta also has the objective and potential to increase financial access and penetration. For investors, the company offers new products to optimize their returns and diversify their portfolio, especially for those that lack access to traditional investment instruments.

Entrepreneur

Alejandro Cosentino, CEO and Founder, is a thought leader in the fintech industry and well-respected in the financial and entrepreneurship sectors in Latin America. He has 30+ years’ experience, combining expertise in financial services and technology, having been in top management positions in financial institutions like American Express and technology/media companies like StarMedia.

Elevar's Take

Scalable, client oriented company that has the potential of impacting the lives of millions through a disruptive and innovative fintech model

Afluenta is Elevar’s third investment from Elevar Equity III in Latin America and follows the fund’s thesis of addressing underserved communities through viable and scalable business models. The availability of data between individuals and financial services providers and the transaction ease offered by modern technology presents great opportunity for innovation to meet the needs of many consumers, underserved otherwise, with high-quality financial services. Afluenta is a unique opportunity for Elevar to back a disruptive business led by strong founding team with a unique combination of financial services, internet/technology and entrepreneurship. Afluenta offers a disruptive model that promotes financial inclusion with greater customer focus for borrowers both within and outside the financial system. For those with access to banking products, Afluenta offers greater convenience, transparency and customer care at similar prices – hence better customer value. For those who don’t have access to bank credit, Afluenta offers financial access and lower cost loans than non-bank financial or payday loan companies.
